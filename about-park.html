Is My Park Safe?

In a perfect world this is not a question, but here we are. This web-based application works to serve the greater community of Austin, Texas in an effort to positively effect the lives of its inhabitants. Our aim was to develop an application to provide a spyglass for the public. In order to do that, we coupled Google Maps API and crime data from the Austin Police Department Crime Reports API to streamline the experience for the user.

Safety is an important issue for every human. We are here to provide the necessary tools to quell any worries you may have. So when you ask yourself whether your park is safe, all you must do is search.
